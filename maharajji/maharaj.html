Shri Hit Premanand Ji Maharaj is very famous for Devotee of Lord Krishna. Their behavior is so simple, easy, and sweet that if you listen to their words, you too will become their devotee. Premanand Swami ji Maharaj is from such a family, where his grandfather and father were also Brahmins and they too were immersed in devotion. His entire family has been simple. His father took retirement after some time of devotion and became a monk. His brothers also used to recite Shri Bhagwat Gita and brought glory to the family. His family used to run only through the work of worship. These are some details of Premanand ji Maharaj biography.
